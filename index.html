My Dearest Mom,

On this special day, I find myself searching for words worthy of the love you've given me, yet no words seem enough.

You are the first person who held me before I knew the world, and the one who continues to hold my heart no matter how old I grow. Every sacrifice you made, every sleepless night, every silent prayer, and every act of unconditional love became the foundation upon which my life stands today.

When life becomes difficult, I remember your strength. When I feel lost, I remember your wisdom. When I achieve something meaningful, I know a part of that success belongs to you because you never stopped believing in me.

You have loved me through my mistakes, celebrated my victories as if they were your own, and stood beside me during moments when the world felt too heavy to carry alone.

Mom, you are not just my mother—you are my greatest teacher, my strongest supporter, my safest place, and one of the greatest blessings I will ever receive in this lifetime.

On your birthday, I pray that life returns to you every bit of happiness, peace, and love that you have spent so generously on everyone around you. You deserve a thousand lifetimes of joy for the love you've given in one.

Happy Birthday, Mom. I love you more than words, more than distance, and more than time itself could ever measure.
